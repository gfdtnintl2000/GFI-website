GR Hub

Forming Lives Through the Golden Rule

About GR Hub

GR Hub is a flagship initiative of Grace Foundation International (GFI) designed to nurture character, compassion, and ethical living across all stages of life. Through age-based curricula, community programs, and practical learning resources, GR Hub equips individuals and communities to live out the Golden Rule:

“Do to others as you would have them do to you.” (Luke 6:31)

Vision & Mission

Vision: A transformed society where individuals live with kindness, dignity, and moral responsibility rooted in the Golden Rule.

Mission: To develop and deliver practical, inclusive, and contextually relevant programs that nurture character, beingness, and ethical living across generations.

Programs

GR Kids

A structured curriculum for ages 1–16 that nurtures kindness, empathy, responsibility, and peaceful living through stories, activities, reflection, and guided practice.

GR Youth

Character, identity, mentorship, leadership, and responsible living for adolescents and young adults.

GR Families

Resources for parenting, moral formation, mutual respect, conflict resolution, and intergenerational dialogue.

GR Learning

Bible study materials, ethical reflections, and practical learning resources for everyday life.

GR Conferences & Community Engagement

Workshops, camps, retreats, community dialogues, and training platforms that deepen Golden Rule-centred living.
