Who We Are

Grace Foundation International (GFI) is a grassroots nonprofit organization based in the South Rift region of Kenya, working in Kericho and Bomet counties. We are committed to empowering communities through education, sustainable agriculture, health initiatives, clean energy, and sports development.

Our Vision

To be a transformative force for communities, where every individual has access to opportunity, dignity, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To empower communities through holistic programs in education, agriculture, health, environment, and social welfare, grounded in compassion, inclusion, and accountability.

Leadership

Our team comprises dedicated educators, community leaders, and development experts with a passion for social change.
