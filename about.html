About Us

Grace Foundation International (GFI) is a non-profit organization committed to uplifting communities in the South Rift Region of Kenya. We believe that access to education, sustainable agriculture, clean energy, water, and sports opportunities can significantly transform lives.

Founded with a vision to create self-reliant and dignified communities, GFI partners with local leaders, educators, and volunteers to implement programs that address the root causes of poverty and inequality. Our work is grounded in compassion, justice, and empowerment.

Our Vision

To build a just, empowered, and self-reliant society where every individual has access to quality education, sustainable livelihoods, and a dignified life.

Our Mission

To transform communities in the South Rift Region of Kenya by promoting education, sustainable agriculture, renewable energy, sports development, and access to clean water and sanitation through collaborative partnerships and grassroots empowerment.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our actions and partnerships.
  • Compassion: We are motivated by empathy and a deep commitment to the dignity and well-being of every individual.
  • Empowerment: We believe in equipping individuals and communities to take charge of their own development.
  • Equity and Inclusion: We promote fair opportunities for all, regardless of background, gender, or social status.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to long-term solutions that respect both people and the planet.
  • Collaboration: We thrive through partnerships and community-driven approaches that foster shared ownership.

From the Founder

Grace Foundation International was born out of a deep conviction that lasting change begins within our communities. Having witnessed firsthand the struggles of underserved families in the South Rift Region, I felt compelled to act—not out of charity, but out of solidarity.

Our journey began with small, hopeful steps—supporting children in school, encouraging farmers to try new ideas, and organizing simple sports events to keep the youth engaged. Over time, these seeds of effort began to grow into something more powerful: a movement of dignity, empowerment, and self-reliance.

At GFI, we believe that every individual has potential. Our mission is to help unlock that potential through education, sustainable livelihoods, clean energy, and compassionate partnerships. We are not just building programs—we are nurturing a future rooted in hope and human worth.

Dr. Joseph Kipkemoi Ronoh
Founder & Director
