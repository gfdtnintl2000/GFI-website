Founder’s Message

Grace Foundation International began with a simple conviction: that dignity, opportunity, and peace grow when we care for one another as we would wish to be cared for. Our programs are practical—rooted in schools, farms, health, and youth livelihoods—yet guided by a moral vision of beingness and mutual responsibility.

We invite partners who share this vision to work with us in empowering young people, strengthening families, and building resilient communities across Kenya’s South Rift region and beyond.

Dr. Joseph Kipkemoi Ronoh
Founder & Executive Director
