Grace Foundation International (GFI)

Empowering Communities in South Rift, Kenya

About Us

Grace Foundation International (GFI) is a nonprofit organization working to uplift lives in Kericho and Bomet counties through education, agriculture, renewable energy, and community development.

Our Programs

  • Education support for vulnerable children
  • Sustainable agriculture and demonstration farms
  • Water, sanitation, and hygiene projects
  • Green energy and environmental stewardship
  • Youth sports and talent development
