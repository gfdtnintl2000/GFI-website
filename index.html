Founder’s Message

Grace Foundation International is rooted in the Golden Rule—"Doing to others as we would like done to us". Guided by this timeless principle, we work with communities in the South Rift region of Kenya to support practical initiatives that enhance daily life.

Our pillars are clear: education (scholarships and school support), sustainable livelihoods (dairy, coffee, and avocado value chains), health (community health platforms), and peace-building & youth empowerment (Champions for Peaceful Community). Our flagship Green Dignity Villages model integrates safe housing, clean water, and renewable energy for resilient households.

We invite partners who share this vision of dignity—where every neighbor can learn, work, stay healthy, and live in peace. Together, let’s embody the Golden Rule in ways our children can feel and trust.
