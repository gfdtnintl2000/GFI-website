Our Projects

GFI implements high-impact projects in the areas of education, health, agriculture, youth development, and renewable energy in Kenyaâ€™s South Rift region.

1. Education Sponsorship

We support vulnerable children through school fees, uniforms, mentorship, and access to quality education.

2. Agricultural Empowerment

We provide training, tools, and seeds to smallholder farmers to promote sustainable food production and income generation.

3. Health and Sanitation

Our mobile clinics and sanitation projects aim to improve access to basic healthcare and hygiene facilities in remote communities.

4. Renewable Energy Solutions

We introduce solar lighting and clean energy systems to reduce reliance on harmful fuels and promote environmental sustainability.

5. Youth and Sports Development

Our sports programs and youth forums nurture talent, promote discipline, and provide safe spaces for personal growth.

6. Water Access Projects

We install rainwater harvesting tanks and boreholes to ensure clean and reliable water for schools and households.
