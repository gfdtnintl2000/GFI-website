Green Dignity Villages

Grace Foundation International is spearheading Green Dignity Villages â€” a transformative initiative that provides sustainable, affordable, and climate-smart housing to vulnerable rural communities in Kenya.

These villages integrate renewable energy, clean water, smart sanitation, and homegrown food systems into eco-friendly homes built with compressed stabilized earth blocks (CSEBs). Each unit is equipped with solar power, rainwater harvesting, composting toilets or biodigesters, and vertical gardens.

Designed for women-headed households, persons with disabilities, and climate-affected families, this initiative reimagines rural housing as a catalyst for resilience, dignity, and holistic community development.

Community-Based Mental Health & Psychosocial Support (MHPSS)

This program trains community health workers, builds peer-support groups, and reduces stigma around mental illness. It targets youth and women, integrating care into schools and clinics.

Recycling Economies for Circular Livelihoods

This initiative empowers youth and women through community recycling hubs, upcycling training, and market linkages, creating green jobs and fostering cleaner environments.

Women & Youth Economic Empowerment Platforms

A program that strengthens financial inclusion, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship for rural women and youth through VSLAs, digital hubs, and start-up seed grants.
