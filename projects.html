Our Projects

We are currently running and fundraising for the following key projects:

1. Education for All

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure support to children in underserved areas. We aim to ensure no child is left behind.

2. Sustainable Agriculture

Training local farmers in modern, eco-friendly farming practices including dairy, coffee, and avocado farming. This doubles as a community demo farm.

3. Green Energy Access

Introducing solar-powered systems in schools and rural homes to improve learning and livelihoods while promoting environmental sustainability.

4. Water & Sanitation

Constructing clean water points and improved sanitation facilities to reduce disease and promote hygiene awareness in rural communities.

5. Community Sports and Empowerment

Building sports facilities and organizing youth tournaments to foster peace, talent development, and social inclusion.
