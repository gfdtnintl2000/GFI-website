Get Involved

Join us in transforming lives and empowering communities in the South Rift Region of Kenya. You can support Grace Foundation International in the following ways:

Make a Donation

Your financial support helps fund education, agriculture, clean energy, and more. Every gift makes a difference.

Volunteer With Us

Contribute your time and skills to projects in education, farming, clean water, or youth programs.

Partner With Us

Organizations, churches, and companies are welcome to collaborate with us in building sustainable impact.

To donate or get involved, please contact us directly. Mobile money and bank account details will be shared upon request.
